Get to know Our School Better

Historical background/Location:

Feza Boys' secondary school started to operate in 1998 at Mikocheni with few students and recently is located at Tegeta with about 480 students. Our school campus has been designed to satisfy the major goals of our esteemed instituion; proper growth of our students mentally, physically and academically. we believe that such a focused environment is best way to create good citizens and future leaders of this great nation.

As a result of quality education we provide, our graduates have attained 100% placement in further educational institutions both in Tanzania and around the world.

Education level and Admission:

Feza Boys Secondary School opened as a secondary school from Form I to Form IV and started high school in 2005. Our school admits all kinds of students including the ones having disabilities like the blind, deaf and those crippled in any other way. Feza Boys secondary school is accredited by The Ministry of Education and Vocational Training of Tanzania. The school conducts competitive admission process.

Staff and Subjects:

The school's staff is mainly Tanzanian with a number of foreign volunteering teachers from different countries around the world. The student body is at large Tanzanian with a minority of white and Asian origin students. Our school follows the curriculum as suggested by NECTA.

There are various of subjects taught in our school. they include: Basic Mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry, Kiswahili, Geography, Physics, Civics, History and Islamic knowledge.

Vision and Mission:

Our vision is to rise a new generation equipped with a quality education, leadership skills, National pride, innovation, creativity, self-discipline, character, value and dignity.

Our mission is to provide beeter education that can bring up creative minds and well-mannered citizens with life-long passion for learning, a strong sense of moral responsibility and a welcoming of diversity.
